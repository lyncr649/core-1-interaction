Cousins-Giving

This weekend my cousins in the New York area got togehter in Long Island for a cousins-giving. We've been doing this the past few years as a way to get together. My cousin Anne hosts the dinner at her home and we all travel out for the day. While there, we sat out in the backyard and did facepaint with Annes daughter Tegan and listened to music.

The song Et Moi, Et Moi, Et Moi was playing as we did the facepaint in the backyard. The silly upbeat feeling of the song matched the night quite well. As the evening went on, we all kept our face paint on. Even sitting down to dinner, we had some more serious conversation all the while looking so silly with our faces on. It felt straight out of a Wes Anderson movie.
