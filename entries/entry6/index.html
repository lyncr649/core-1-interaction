Sweet Pickle Books

On Saturday October 22nd, I spent the day with my brother James and my friend Etai. We walked around the Lower East Side, exploring galleries that James's teacher reccomended. While exploring, we passed a pickle bookstore and we just had to go in. While shopping around, I was loving the music they played.

Mind by the Talking Heads was playing as we entered the store.
